SCADA

Supervisory Control And Data Acquisition Systems (SCADA) are the heartbeat of your facility. Keeping your systems updated and secure is a growing challenge in manufacturing. CedarTech can implement Human Machine Interface (HMI) solutions for process control, Historians to view plant history, and everything in between.

MES

Leveraging a Manufacturing Execution System (MES) is the key to integrating data across all units of your business. Managing various processes across teams sometimes dictates a need for custom solutions. CedarTech can assist you with Recipe Management, ERP Integration, Track and Trace, and more.

Reporting

Do you know how well your facility is running? Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE) gives you an accurate view by correlating availability, performance, and quality. Need to see more data? We can create custom reports integrating historical process data, labor costs, downtime issues, and more.
